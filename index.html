My Experience of NDA Entrance Examination and SSB Interview
My Experiences


In my Junior College, I started the preparations for the NDA Examination to fulfill my dream of joining the forces. It was my first experience
of giving a National Level Competitive Examination as it is conducted by the UPSC. But I was fortunate that I was able to qualify the 
Examination in my first attempt and I was called for the Services Selection Board Interview at Bhopal.

Just for a matter of fact, it is interesting to know that the NDA examination is one of the toughest examinations in India and the SSB 
interview is India's toughest interview !!! The SSBs are conducted for 5 days comprising of Stage-I on the first day and the final selection
is done on the last day.

I started preparing for the interview and it was to be in April, 2020 but due to COVID-19 lockdown it was suspended. So, I got a good time to
prepare for it and get to know what happens in this interview. Later, my interview was scheduled in July,2020. The Stage-I of it is normally 
conducted on the first day and I was one of the 18 candidates qualified out of the 70 candidates reported. But unfortunately, I was not able to
clear the last Stage of the interview process. 

I felt low at one time but I knew that I have one more chance to achieve my dream. So, again I started my preparations for my 2nd
attempt and meanwhile my score card also came for my first attempt which was 666 out of 1800 and at that time the cut off was 709 for the 
selection. By this I decided that I will improve the next time. Later, I again qualified the examination and was called for the interview again
at Bhopal. I qualified the Stage-I but again I didn't qualify the last round. This time my marks were 775 out of 1800 and the cut off was 696. 
But due to some less marks in my SSB, I did not clear the interview. 

In this chapter of my life, I will never forget the life and the discipline I experienced during my 10 days stay at the Selection Boards. I have 
also got up at 4 am for the interview process !!! It was a good phase as I got to know about how an interview is conducted at a young age. 
Above all, it has increased my desire to join the Indian Army as I also want to "Live a Life Less Ordinary."



My Engineering
My Experiences



When I didn't get selected for NDA, I gave the MHT-CET Examination and I was able to get admission in Computer Engineering branch at
Dr. D Y Patil Institute of Engineering, Management and Research. Till today, my experience with DYPEMR is fantastic. In my First Year,
I gave the NEO Olympiad 4.0 and my national rank was 3055. After giving that Olympiad, I also got an internship opportunity at 
Internship Studio. I chose to do internship in Artificial Intelligence. In that Experience, I got to know how the apps which we use in 
our daily life use AI. The most interesting part of the internship was that I was given an assignment of German to English translation.
Overall, it was a good internship experience.

In my 2nd semester, we had Project Based Learning in which we had to create projects on any topic and explain it in our batch.
So, I was included in a six-member team. We all decided to make a project on Cryptography along with a sample program and I was chosen 
as the team leader. So, on my side my duties were to allocate the sub-topics of the project to each and every member as per their capabilities.
I also made sure that the team works in good harmony and make sure that rehearsals and the project and program completion was done
in time. Finally, on the day we presented well and answered all the questions that were asked to us. In that phase, I got to know how
important it is for a team to coordinate and function well in order to achieve a goal. 

I have also done a certification course in Cybersecurity and Its Ten Domains through Coursera, which was offered by The University System of Georgia,
in which I got to know that how important is the data for a company and what are the measures to be taken to secure the private data 
of an industry.

In my second year, I have come into the World of Computers!! In the beginning itself, I got to know many new things in the field of Programming.
Here too, I participated in many competitions like an Essay and Poster Competition on the auspicious occasion of Engineer's Day. I wrote on the topics of 
"Social Media : A boon for Students" and "The real self-fulfillment of Entrepreneurs." I also attended many webinars like "Computer Graphics using Maya",
"Good Programming Standards", and so on.

In the future too, I will be participating in many more such webinars ,competitions and so on. I hope to gain many new experiences and learnings which will be
very beneficial for my future.
